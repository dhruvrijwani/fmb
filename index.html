Overview of The Program

WeSchool's PGP in Entrepreneurship and Family Managed Business (EFMB)(PGP-Entrepreneurship Management explores and analyzes the best management, governance practices, and family continuity challenges, including issues relating to first generation entrepreneurs.

The Program structure is based on AAA (Acquisition, Application & Assimilation of Knowledge). The PGP in Entrepreneurship and Family Managed Business (EFMB) consists of two Semesters spread over a period of 11 months. In every month, one week is devoted to Acquisition of Knowledge, three weeks to Application of Knowledge in one's own business and one day for Assimilation of Knowledge.

What is the meaning of entrepreneurship management?

Entrepreneurship management is the process of planning, organizing, directing, and controlling the activities of an entrepreneurial venture. It involves identifying business opportunities, developing a business plan, acquiring necessary resources, and leading the execution of the venture.

What is the duration of the PG in Entrepreneurship & Family Business Management program?

The Post Graduate program in Entrepreneurship & Family Business Management with a post-graduate diploma in entrepreneurship management is designed to be completed in 11 months. This intensive duration includes a comprehensive curriculum that combines classroom learning with practical experiences, ensuring that you gain the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in entrepreneurship and manage family businesses effectively.

What are the key benefits of this program?

This program offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to equip students with practical skills and knowledge relevant to the industry. Key benefits include exposure to real-world challenges, opportunities for hands-on learning through internships and projects, access to experienced faculty and industry professionals, and a strong network of alumni and industry connections. Additionally, the program emphasizes leadership development, critical thinking, and adaptability, preparing graduates for success in a dynamic and competitive global market.

What are the eligibility criteria for applying?

To apply for the PG in Entrepreneurship & Family Managed Business Management, applicants must have a graduate degree from a recognized university and be actively involved in their family business, preferably as a second-generation member or beyond

Are there any specific academic or professional prerequisites?

While there are no formal academic or professional requirements, having a bachelor’s degree and work experience can make your application stronger and help you grasp the course material better.

Can I pursue this program part-time or online?

This program is designed to be completed on a full-time, in-person basis. Currently, there are no part-time or online options available.
